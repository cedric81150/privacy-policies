1. Introduction

MealPlanner ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile meal planning application developed by C√©dric Braun. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

2. Data Collection

MealPlanner is designed with "privacy by default" principles. We do not collect any personally identifiable data.

2.1 Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Recipes: Titles, ingredients, instructions, images you create
  • Weekly Menus: Your meal planning
  • Shopping Lists: Items generated from your menus
  • Preferences: Theme, language, accessibility settings
  • Premium Status: Information about your in-app purchase

2.2 Data NOT Collected

We do NOT collect:

  • Any personal information (name, email, address)
  • Any location data
  • Any analytics or tracking data
  • Any cookies or trackers

3. Camera Usage

The app requests camera access only to scan QR codes for sharing recipes between devices. Captured images are not saved and never leave your device.

4. In-App Purchases

MealPlanner offers a one-time "Premium" purchase via Google Play or Apple App Store. Transactions are handled exclusively by Google/Apple according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

We do not receive or store any payment information. Only an anonymous transaction identifier is kept locally to validate your purchase.

5. Recipe Sharing (QR Code)

The QR code recipe sharing feature encodes your recipes in a format readable only by the app. This sharing is:

  • Local (no intermediate server)
  • Voluntary only
  • Without transmission of personal data

6. Third-Party Services

MealPlanner does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Data Security

Since your data is stored exclusively on your device, its security depends on your operating system's protections (Android/iOS). We recommend:

  • Using a lock code on your device
  • Keeping your system up to date
  • Performing regular device backups

8. User Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible in the app
  • Modification: You can edit or delete any data
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all your local data
  • Portability: Export your recipes via QR code or PDF

9. Children's Privacy

MealPlanner is suitable for all ages. Since no data is collected, there is no risk for minors. However, we recommend parental supervision for young users.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new update date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. GDPR Compliance

MealPlanner complies with the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). By not collecting any personal data, we eliminate any risk of non-compliance.
